

* open Visual studio

* click on the platformio bug icon

* find main.cpp

* click Build upload

* onboard the device:
    https://console.thethingsnetwork.org/applications/mitems

(All using OTAA mode)

Copy out chipid from Monitor output, add two zero bytes at front to make it 8 bytes total:
    00 00 70 84 fe a4 ae 30

put into Device EUI of Things network
Give it a deviceID: ttgo_oled4
click on [register]

* show the App Key, put into C++ mode, copy into source code of device in main.cpp
